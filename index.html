About Me

I am a young, energetic techie in the Kenyan Tech scene who is not only passionate about building great products, but also a guy who enjoys helping the community. I am a graduate of JKUAT, BSc. Mathematics and Computer Science, class of 2018. Besides the degree, I also possess several certificates in Data Analytics, Cyber Security, Data Science and Engineering.

Why Tech

From a young age, both at home and school, I was always fascinated by computers. All my leisure time was spent in the company of computers playing games, browsing, etc. This passion led me to pursue studies in computers.
As for me, I wanted to be able to digitise processes in our country since most services in this country are done physically thus making service delivery inefficient. I also want to build projects that help my community. With these as my drivers, I took the tech route since I was well conversant with the computer world.
To date, I have been able to do this, though at a small scale, and my aim is to improve the lives of Kenyans via tech products.
